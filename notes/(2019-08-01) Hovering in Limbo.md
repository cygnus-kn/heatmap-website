I have never believed in faith, especially that of other all-knowing omnipresent, omnipotent consciousness. Had there been such deity, it would obsolete itself out of existence in the absence of meaningfulness. what could have been, what have been, what are, what could be, what will, whatever, are governed by the dynamic of interaction between primordially fundamental forces, physically matter, and socially animals, which including us. 

Presumably there is such an accurate prediction of future, and such prediction is accessed and acted upon, then, it would not hold. Chain of event altering in the face of its prediction ensures no prediction exists in the first place - a type-2 chaos system whereas type-1 typically stays its course regardlessly (i.e: the swing of an pendulum in a closed system, orbit of 02 celestials within a closed system )

But recent chain of event casts doubt in that very system of believe, one which has been reinforced by observation and actual experience. Acknowledging that randomness means anomalies shall inevitably carry itself out. And that there being bias nature, warped perception, intuitive tendency of human to attribute meaningfulness to a chain of event in an attempt to comprehend or at least, to cope with such situation. The fact that every single major events leading up to the final decision facilitates it implies  intendedness. Maybe, this is a singularity whirlpooling previous decisions into one path. Alternatively, there may be limited set of actions that would result in this singularity. Or simply, I am just too stressed out, tired, insomnia and severely lack time to take into full account of what actually happened. Occam's razor tells that the later hold the highest probability. 

The problem of extreme skeptical attitude toward one's arrival of conclusion is the inability to draw any concrete lessons that could be helpful thereafter. Uncertainty of correlation and causation to the issue further complicates this process. Even a loop of constant self-feedback does little when each cycle is shrouded in indetermination of actual event vs reported event as well as actual implied dynamics vs self-interpreted dynamics. 

Torment assured, escapism tempting, logical process shut down.
