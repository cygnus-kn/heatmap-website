The weather is so nice today! I headed out to my class and I can see that the sky is just like a painting, with clear blue background and brushes of cloud, like an artist gently puts his brush and makes a swirly little cloud. Then dip some pinkish and orange paint on the horizon. The birds also notices such nice weather and join to the sky as they soar their wings. It reminds me of the covid days, when I was locked down at home, with no contact to the outside, beside my windows. On the afternoon like this, with the same weather, it evokes in me, the same emotion.
