During S127, I've organized a pretty success activity when it comes to engagement level as well as student's learning.

> Firstly, I have them wrote out 5 topics that they've learned and worked in pairs to answer those questions. They would randomly choose a topic and their teammate had to answer it in 30s, this is so that they can start practicing a variety of topics, two people per pair means at least 10 different topics although there may be some replicate.

> Secondly, I divided the class into 2 teams, and collected back all the paper with all the topics (around 40 topics) and told them that I would give each team 4 topics, they can prepare it together in 5 minutes.

> After 5 minutes, I will ask one person from each to come up to the board and randomly chose a topic for them to speak. Since no one knows what topic they will get, they have to learn all 4 of it. Then for each good answer, I'll give them point base on the performance of each member.

To increase the difficulty of this game, I can just give them more topics to prepare in advance within the same 5 minutes, so they have to make it quicker.
