I've just been back from VT city in the weekend. They have been doing a lot of construction lately. It's one of my biggest fears that I would not recognize the city where I was born once it's been under too many major changes. A lot of them are just terrible for the people living there. For example. all the roundabouts was replaced by traffic lights, which makes the city less unique, there used to be many famous roundabout in VT city, all of them are demolished, stripped of their identity and left as a soulless green and red light. Now there are major road constructions going on, which expand old streets, where there are lot of businesses and shades, and turn it into major road fitting for cars. Financially, it makes sense, biggest roads mean house along its side gain higher value, but it strips away the business friendly sideway, the convenience of crossing the street, and the shades from the trees. Oh, they cut down the trees too, to make room for more road. Now it looks like a scorching place, the sun reflect one the street into anyone who would drive by. I sincerely think they are trying to ruin VT as a city.
