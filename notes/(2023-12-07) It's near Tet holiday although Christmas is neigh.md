I ate a lot for breakfast and so I thought to have light dinner with some porridge. The lunch nap was good but I was woken up with so many notifications from my phone. I saw a person transporting some sunflower today so it feels like Tet holiday is near, but it's only near Christmas. Christmas has, in the past, been not that popular in Vietnam but now, it's everywhere. I doubt that it has spiritual value to the majority of Vietnamese and more of a manufactured custom to sell more things. Although I do believe it has value to the Christian community here in Vietnam. As with every holiday. I feel a sense of nostalgia, thinking about last year's or last decade's holiday and how it turns out. Actually, Christmas became something matter was in secondary school, when I have an internet connection and was able to roam the online space, people were celebrating Christmas and made plans to go out, especially to churches to see some music festival. I distinctly remember going to one but then went back to my home to play some helicopter attacking game. It might be two completely different Christmases but the memory kinda meshes together.
