Oh ye old sore throat, how I loath thy return. Yes, I got sore throat again, it started as just an innocuous runny nose that blew into a sore throat in the middle of the night. One theory I've got is that eating excessively during onset of sore throat could actually accelerate it. I had some chicken burger last night and it definitely makes it worse. So next time when I smell the sore throat coming, I can just stop eating and see what happen. 

I stayed at home all day, only occasional went outside to buy food and medicine for the sore throat. The weather is cold, but there is another interesting thing, that because there were no wind, the air pollution was the highest ... actual HCMC air quality was in top 4 most polluted today, which took me by surprise. I guess my sore throat was both a curse and a blessing. 

Another notable thing that happened today was that there was a fire near my place, just inside the traditional Giong Ong To market in the morning, they blocked off the road for a few hours in the morning for the fire fighters to come to put it down.
