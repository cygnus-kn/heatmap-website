**A small note for February**

I am glad that I’m heading to the right direction. Since the day I left my previous desk job, life have been a little unstable for a while. I was strapped for cash and it was a rough start going into teaching. But right now, everything is alright, I got more classes and the performance, I felt, are OK now. Few month ago, I would be really nervous whenever going to class, it’s better now. There are still many improvements that I’d like to make. Namely, how to warm up the class, get them into a hyped-up mindset before the lesson. There is also the goal of teaching IELTS, whose first step is to take the IELTS test itself. I am really lazy to put effort in studying it in my free time. But, it’s needed to be done soon. Financially stable has helped tremendously with my anxiety, which is something that would affect my teaching job more than a desk job. There are much to be learned, hopefully, I’m able to at lease, involve in IELTS teaching this year. There is a sense of “time is running out”, not too fast, but noticeable. This opposes my relaxed and carefree attitude in the morning, when I’m supposed to put effort in furthering goals. Perhaps, I should do that one exercise of “Getting Things Done” something in the near future.
