Sunlight is scorching through the apartment windows brings radiated heat that rises up the bed which lies on the upper part of the house. It's unbearable, hostile to anyone who wish to stay for the afternoon. It drives me outside every afternoon after 02:00 P.M. Air conditioner, previously oblivious to the living experience of a college student, suddenly become a necessity to recent graduate, at least to those who decide to take a white collar job.

Therefore, in the mid afternoon of a hot Sunday , I stay in one of those coffee shop under the comfort of air-conditioning and a fresh cold drink to write above whatever I want.

An update to Year of Extension and Year of Sustainability is called for. To say that up to this point, "total failure" is an accurate description for any advancement in whichever project. There are certain inertia-inducing actors around. "There was an attempt" should be the appropriate subtext for January to April.

To further Year of Sustainability, I have tried to cut down on plastic bag, which meets with lots of resistance, from street food vendors. Plastic bag is the utmost of convenience for any takeaway and are used for everything, to a point of redundancy. In one instance, I had 02 items: banh mi and other baked bread with stuffing and each would be wrapped in a plastic bag and then both are put in a third one. This absurd amount of packaging are justified for convenience and sanitary. Other instance are no less wasteful with at least more than 02 layers of packaging for just an item of food that would then be consumed in less than 15 minutes. The state of breakfast is broken, and an only real viable solution is home-prepared food. Which I was always hesitate to implement for planning and logistics reason that could be later explained in details. There must be more information on how to become more sustainable and this is exactly what I am going to do, searching for information of how people go green and how to consume less plastic and wasteful material.

There was also an attempt at the Year of Extension where I searched and interviewed for various places with no real result. It drains more energy because the active searching and keeping up with various job position openings and application status is an on-going background task that consumes a lot of my willpower. However, this is an importance task to be done, but right now, I am not actively looking for anything, let April passes and then do it again.

There has been less activities both online and irl that draws myself further to the introversion side spectrum, this must be fixed by expanding activities outside of gaming.

This holiday at the end of April has been planned months in advance, hopefully the retrieval+planning trip will be productive. I would like to get many things done in that holiday to put more drive to implement plans for the rest of the year.

There is a project that is unfinished from last year, which is IELTS test, it was terminated on the basis that there is no merit to taking that test, I am half-heartedly want to resume that project when further evaluation is done.

This has been a short update for April.
