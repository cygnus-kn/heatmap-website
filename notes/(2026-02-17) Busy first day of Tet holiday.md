It's the first day of Tet holiday this year and it started busy. Yesterday, I slept at around 1:50 p.m so it is irritating when I had to wake up early because of all the sound my parents made when cooking vegetarian food to prepare to the meal offering today.

Luckily, the cooking was lighter than yesterday because we only need to offer two tables to my grandparents and to an uncle with 5 vegetarian dishes. Both my parents has cooked most of the dishes before 7:00 a.m and I only need to bring them to the altar and light up the incense to call forth the spirit of ancestors to come and enjoy to offered meals.

Then guests from my father side started coming in waves, three to be specific. With the first one coming early, even before 9:00 a.m. For both the later waves, we have to serve food and drink to them, which is an exhausted effort to do and clean up after. Everything stopped at around 11:30 when everyone left, by then, the weather is so hot and windy. Both my parents decided to visit grandparents from my mother's side but I stayed at home to take some needed rest. There are so many children that I needed to give lucky money to that I think I'll run out of them on the second day of Tet.

I slept until 4:00 p.m then at 5:00 p.m I came to my grandparents from my mother's side. Most of the aunts and uncles has left for their journey to visit 10 temples in Dong Nai province so I gathers some siblings to give lucky money to as well as going to a coffee shop to play blackjack. They took so long to prepare and they also had dinner first before going, it wasn't until nearly 7 p.m that we started to depart.

We went to a cafe overlooking the beach and had a blast playing blackjack, everyone having fun and the banter was getting so loud that at one point, I was afraid we were going the get kick out but fortunately no. We went home at around 9:00 p.m and I had some instant noodle with egg, shrimp and veggie for dinner since I haven't eaten anything since 4:00 p.m

It was a tiring day but it's fun, especially the playing blackjack part. 

Tomorrow, I will have to visit some of my relatives from my father's side to give them wishes for Tet holiday, so tomorrow will be somewhat busy.
