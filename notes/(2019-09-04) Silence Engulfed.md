It is late, perhaps the later part of the day when my mental capacity is at its lowest point, when what anyone want to do is to sit back and consume, may it be contents from the internet, contents from games, books or any activities that requires nothing from self and anything from creators.

If I'd be real for a second and look at my to-do list, I'd enrage in anxiety attack which then set off a circle of hell that will perpetuate till morning, when said to-do must at least started. The description sounds like a withdrawal symptom but it is actually a defense mechanism.

From what ? - whispered the voices in my head. Well, from the burden of responsibilities that is ensured to follow and guaranteed to prolonged to insurmountable amount of time.

Fact is, the longer my existence .. uh..uhm.. exists, choices are no longer options but compromises where a path would effectively eliminate possibility of taking another. What seem like opportunity were shadowed with simultaneous negation of commitment to other. Suddenly, decisions become not only for what to have, but also what to have-not. Whereas the have-not is certain, the have is risk-ridden.

In the face of uncertainty, due to whether of genuine lack of information or incompetence body, decision maker inevitably delays, then regrets, then rationalizes, then accepts whatever outcome played out. The only action that impacts is decisions themselves.

To-do list, therefore, become such as hedge fund to investment. To tell/predict/project what should happen despite what would happen, the asymmetry between which is determined by expectation, action (determinant) and the collective cluster referred to as "opportune moment/advantageous position" (variant), though other might insist the opposite, that "opportune moment/advantageous position" is determinant whereas expectation, action is adjustable, therefore, variant. The point of which is worthless.

What then, does any of it makes, if, despite the best effort to hedge the future, it overflows. What then left, if not silence engulfed. What then, in a function of determinant and variant, the later exceedingly but inconspicuously determine the result.
