It's the second day of Tet holiday, the wind picked up even stronger than yesterday, my porch got dirt on its moments after I swept them. In the morning, I went to visit some of my relatives on my father's side and give them Tet wishes. Then I went to my grandparents' house from my mother's side to play some blackjack. I lost few thousands as per usual but it's tradition. At around 11:30 a.m, I visited one of the aunt one my father's side to have a get-together, oh, and it's tradition, been doing it for the last 20 years. 

I eventually went back home to take a rest until 5:30 pm when I went to another uncle from my mother's side for another get-together, we had some steamed chicken, cured meat and Chinese-style chicken soup. It's fulfilling, they start doing karaoke and playing blackjack. I am a little bit tired of the routine so I opted to went home early, not before wandering around the beach on my scooter a little bit. There is a fair going on at the newly built park along the beach, it was completed before the merger of different provinces and it is really a tourist trap, there were traffic jam whenever I approach an entrance for motorbike parking because too many people waiting to get in that it overflowed onto the street blocking traffic.

The monotonous is starting to set in. I hope to do something else tomorrow.
