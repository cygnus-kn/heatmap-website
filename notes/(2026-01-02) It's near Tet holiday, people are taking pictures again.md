I went out on an excursion today, taking bus 153 to Metro line 1 and arrive at Ba Son station. The park overlooking the Saigon river hear are being decorated with an assortment of flowers and colorful installation. Lots of people wearing traditional clothings are going on the Metro to the city center to take picture also. It feels like Tet, again, is nearer than it actually does.

On some of my recent trip using the bus, I noticed that children here is Ho Chi Minh are surprisingly independent. They just take the bus without any adult accompanying them. For example today, I saw 3 children aged somewhat in primary school taking the bus together and hanging out, something that I thought was reckless if it was happening in my time. But I guess my time was old, I was in primary school in 2005. Another cool thing is that most of them looks like they belong to the working class, with their clothings and demeanor. The children of a more middle class family was always accompanied by their parents though, they are really protective of them, especially in city like this. So it's cool to see such a contrast of upbringing between social classes.
